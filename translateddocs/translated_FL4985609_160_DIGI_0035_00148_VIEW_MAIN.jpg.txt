NOTICE

The regular Review Meeting (conscripts from Belgium born between
1881 and 1900) will take place on February 3rd at 10 o'clock in the morning.
In light of the ordinance issued by the German authorities,
all persons born from 1872 to 1880 inclusive must also attend.

For these people, the Review Meeting will take place on February 3rd
at 2:30 in the afternoon.

The Municipal Authority.

Merelebeke, February 26, 1917.

Printed by St. Antonius, Jos. Dooreman-Volekaert.
