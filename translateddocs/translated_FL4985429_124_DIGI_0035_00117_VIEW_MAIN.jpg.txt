"To the municipality, it is hereby forbidden to sell any quantity of potatoes to traders and individuals without the permission of the Command Post, except to those traders who possess a certificate from the Command Post.

This order is to be immediately communicated to all residents in the municipality and remains in force until another regulation is announced.

Infractions will be punished along with the confiscation of the potatoes.

The Etappen-Commandant

(signed) Wettstein

Lieutenant."
