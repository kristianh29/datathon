"Mobile Stage Command, 231st E.H.O., 1 December 1916.

Agricultural Operations Office, Reg. IV, L. 2367.

= Voluntary Planting of EARLY POTATOES for 1917, is ordered herewith, due to a limited supply by the municipalities:

1. Each landowner must plant 10% of their fields designated for potatoes with early potatoes.

A detailed table of the surface area to be cultivated must be submitted to the Agricultural Operations Office of the Command by 5 December.

2. The municipality is to instruct all gardeners to cultivate as few ornamental plants and shrubs as possible, and instead plant early potatoes in those areas.

The Stage Commander,

Printed by Sint-Antonius, Jos. Dooreman-Volekaert."
