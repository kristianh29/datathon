"On behalf of the Etappen-Kommandantur, the following announcements are made:

1. It is henceforth forbidden to sell chickens.

2. Military personnel who are not in possession of a permit from the Kommandantur are not entitled to make purchases from farmers, this applies to all livestock, chickens and rabbits, as well as hay, straw, leather, or other rationed goods.

3. If in the future thefts of livestock still occur, without the perpetrator or the stolen items being discovered, the victims will be held responsible and themselves subject to punishments and fines."
