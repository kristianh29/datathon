Mob. Etappen-Kommandantur, Ghent, the 6th of September 1916.

The Scheldt meadows between Merelbeke - Nederzwijnaarde

Terhand and the road Ghent-St. Pieters - Denderleeuw, may

not be entered from 7:30 to 8:45

as entering this place during that time is life-threatening.

The Etappen-Kommandant,

_ Printing. Sint-Antonius, Jos. Dooreman-Volckaert.
