The Traffic of Butter

The Administrative Chiefs for Flanders and Wallonia have, in execution of Article 10 of the regulation of the Herr General-Governor of August 22, 1916, concerning the traffic of butter, set the quantity allowed to the producer for personal use, until further notice, at

100 grams per person
per week

This regulation comes into effect on August 1, 1917; anyone who does not comply will expose themselves to the penalties mentioned in the sole article of the regulation of October 18, 1916.

Antwerp, July 30, 1917.

The President of the Civil Administration

for the Province of Antwerp

SCHRAMM

Senator.
