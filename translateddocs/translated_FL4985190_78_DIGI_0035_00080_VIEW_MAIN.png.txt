At the Austro-Hungarian Commissariat in Brussels
a war precaution office is being formed, connected with
the Austro-Hungarian Assistance Association (Antwerp-
enstone road No. 125) to assist Austro-Hungarian citizens
with advice and support.

Consultation hours at the Assistance Office (Montoyerstraat, 24),
Monday and Thursday from 3 to 6 o'clock; in urgent cases
at all times.
