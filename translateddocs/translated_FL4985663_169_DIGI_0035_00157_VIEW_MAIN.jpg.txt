"Municipality of Merelbeke

From this week onward, meat will only be sold on Saturday

and at the following hours:

in the morning from 8 to 12:30 - in the afternoon from 3 to 5:30.

We remind once again that complaints regarding rationing will only be accepted on Tuesday, at the Town Hall, in the morning from 9 to 11.

Merelbeke, 20 February 1917

Printed by St. Antonius, Jos. Dooreman-Valocke"
