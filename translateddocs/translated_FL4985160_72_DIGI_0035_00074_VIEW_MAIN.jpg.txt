"ORDINANCE,

regarding

/

regulation of the butter trade.

Sole article.

The Ordinance of 1st February 1916
concerning the modification and supplement
of the Ordinance of 30th November 1915 on
the regulation of the butter trade (Law and
Ordinance Gazette p. 1625) established
maximum butter prices remain provisionally

in force.

Freiherr VON BISSING,
