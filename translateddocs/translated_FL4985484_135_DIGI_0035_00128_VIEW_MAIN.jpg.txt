The Mayor of Meirelbeke.

In view of the regulation no. 233. E. Fi. O. 25. 9-16, from Mr. Etappen-Inspector von Unger, General of the Cavalry,

In view of the renewed order of the Occupying Power:

Considering articles 90, 94, and 102 of the municipal law, It is decided:

transport and trade of potatoes, without permission from the Etappen-Command, is prohibited.

The offenders will have their goods confiscated, and declared forfeited, furthermore they will be punished with police penalties. This decision is immediately executable.

Meirelbeke 1916 16. T1316. | Jos
