"The conscription assembly of Belgian men born in 1896 from the municipality of Mellebeke will take place on July 8, 1916, at 9:30 in the morning in the town hall.

All conscripted men from this municipality must appear without exception. Failure to comply with this order or arriving late will be punished with imprisonment without the possibility of appeal.

Mellebeke, [date not provided].

The Mayor,

(unknown signature)

Secretary-Saint Anthony, Jos. Dooreman-Volckaert."
