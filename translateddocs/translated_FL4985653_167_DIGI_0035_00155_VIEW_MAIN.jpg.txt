"COMMUNE MEIRELEKE

By order of the Etappen Command, the Commune must deliver all milk to the Dairy in Lemberge starting Saturday, 10 February 1917. It is understood that farmers must bring their milk to the Town Hall every morning before 7 o'clock, as the delivery men regularly depart at 7:15.

The cans and bins must also be sealed. Galvanized cans are refused.

It is again urged that ALL COW OWNERS must deliver their milk.

Meirelbeke, the 8th of February 1917.

Printed by Sint-Antonius, Jos. Dooreman"
