Mobile Etappen-Kommandantur SS E. H. O., 11-12-16.

_ 287 - 8, A. K. - of the 4th Army. - J. No. 4894.

The arms officers have received instructions today regarding the postal traffic with civilian workers. It emerges from this that the family members of the requisitioned persons of the Z. A. B. can deliver packages to the Kommandantur, Kouter No. 12, on a specific day each month.

Two days before the delivery of the packages, they must indicate the number of requested parcel addresses, which are provided here free of charge to the mail carrier.

The Etappe Kommandantur,

Print. St. Antonius, Jos. Dooreman-Volekaert.
