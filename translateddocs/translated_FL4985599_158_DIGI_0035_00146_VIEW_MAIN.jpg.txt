The Mayor of Meirelbeke;

- In view of art. 94 of the Municipal Law | and the announcement | of the Provincial Food Committee,
Decrees:

Art. 1. Starting from Sunday, January 7, 1917, all the flour delivered by the Committee to the bakers will be charged at 48.65 francs per 100 kilos.
Art. 2. The price of bread is set at 0.42 francs per loaf of 925 grams.

Meirelbeke, January 10, 1917.

The Mayor, Jos. Hebbelynck.

Sale at St. Antony, by Dooreman-Volekaert.
