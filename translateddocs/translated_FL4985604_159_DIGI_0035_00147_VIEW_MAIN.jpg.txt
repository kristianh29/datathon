The translation of the text is:

"E.H.O., January 23, 1917.

The Mayors are to announce to their residents
that for a turf factory in Westphalia (Germany), 10 worker families
and 25 male and female servants are wanted for good wages.

Furthermore, a large number of male and female servants are wanted for
agriculture in Germany.

Further information can be obtained at the rental office for craftsmen,
Command Headquarters, Kouter 28.

The municipalities must report by 26.1.17 how the above
has been made known to the population."
